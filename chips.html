Homemade Potato Chips

by Jeff Wojcik

Ingredients

  • 7 unpeeled medium potatoes (about 2 pounds)
  • 2 quarts ice water
  • 5 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons celery salt
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons pepper
  • Oil for deep-fat frying

Directions

Step 1: Using a vegatable peeler or metal cheese slicer, cut potatoes into very thin slices. Place in a large bowl; add ice water and salt. Soak for 30 minutes.

Step 2: Drain potatoes; place on paper towels and pat dry. In a small bowl, combine the garlic powder, celery salt, and pepper; set aside.

Step 3: In a cast-iron or other heavy skillet, heat 1-1/2 in. oil to 375 degrees. Fry potatoes in batches until golden brown, 3-4 minutes, stirring frequently.

Step 4: Remove with a slotted spoon; drain on paper towels. Immediately sprinkle with seasoning mixture. Store in an airtight container.
